Job Title: Marketing Specialist

Location: Portland, Oregon

Job Type: Full-Time

Company Overview: At BrightWave Solutions, we are dedicated to delivering innovative digital solutions that transform businesses and enhance customer experiences. As a leader in the tech industry, we believe in the power of creativity and data-driven marketing strategies to drive growth and build strong brand loyalty. We are currently seeking a talented and enthusiastic Marketing Specialist to join our dynamic team.

Position Overview: As a Marketing Specialist at BrightWave Solutions, you will be responsible for designing and executing marketing campaigns that boost our brand visibility, engage our audience, and support our business objectives. You will collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop impactful strategies that drive our brand forward. The ideal candidate will bring both a creative flair and a results-oriented mindset to our marketing efforts.

Key Responsibilities:

Campaign Development & Execution: Design and implement integrated marketing campaigns across digital channels including social media, email marketing, and paid advertising.
Content Creation: Partner with the content team to produce engaging content for blogs, social media, newsletters, and other marketing channels.
Social Media Management: Manage and grow BrightWave Solutions’ social media presence by curating, scheduling, and publishing posts, while engaging with followers to build a loyal online community.
Market Research & Analysis: Conduct research to stay updated on industry trends, customer preferences, and competitor activities, and use these insights to refine marketing strategies.
Performance Tracking & Reporting: Analyze key performance metrics from marketing campaigns to assess effectiveness and identify areas for optimization.
SEO & SEM Strategy: Assist in optimizing the company’s website and managing paid search campaigns to increase traffic and lead generation.
Event Planning: Support marketing events such as product launches, webinars, and conferences, and ensure effective promotion and follow-up for each event.
Required Skills & Qualifications:

Bachelor’s Degree in Marketing, Communications, or a related field.
2-4 years of experience in a marketing role (digital marketing preferred).
Strong knowledge of digital marketing tools such as Google Analytics, HubSpot, and social media management platforms.
Familiarity with SEO best practices, Google Ads, and Facebook Ads.
Exceptional communication skills and attention to detail.
Ability to manage multiple projects in a fast-paced environment.
Analytical mindset with a focus on using data to inform marketing decisions.
Preferred Qualifications:

Experience in B2B marketing or technology sectors.
Familiarity with graphic design tools like Canva or Adobe Creative Suite.
What We Offer:

Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits.
Opportunities for professional growth and career advancement.
A collaborative, innovative work culture.
Flexible work schedule and remote work options.
Health, dental, and vision insurance, along with a 401(k) plan and paid time off.
How to Apply: If you are passionate about marketing and want to help us elevate BrightWave Solutions to new heights, please submit your resume and cover letter to careers@brightwave.com by October 31st.
